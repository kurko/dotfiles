COPILOT + MUCOMPLETE
--------------------
To enable Copilot and disable MUcomplete auto popups:
    :CopilotOn

To disable Copilot and use MUcomplete as usual:
    :CopilotOff

<Tab> will:
- Accept Copilot suggestion if visible
- Cycle MUcomplete menu if open
- Otherwise insert a tab or trigger MUcomplete
